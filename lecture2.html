About Us

NUA BIKES – HANDMADE IN BARCELONA

We give a little insight into the product range of Nua Bikes.

In general, Nua Bikes offers numerous models and (currently) five Pinion frames for various applications. While the Nua Dual and Nua Kensho models are designed for fast, sporty progress in urban surroundings, the Nua Roure, Nua Salvaje and Nua La Bestia are made for travel, adventure and bike packing. If you don’t find what you are looking for in this basic configuration, Nua Bikes offers the implementation of almost all ideas within the framework of “Customers Special Projects”…

1.) Special project: Kevins Porteur is based on the Roure geometry.

2.) Dual Commuter – This built is equipped with a fixed mudguard and Supernova lighting for year-round use.

WE CREATE GEARBOX TECHNOLOGY FOR HIGHEST DEMANDS

Centrally integrated into the bicycle frame, Pinion gearboxes offer unique riding dynamics and unparalleled shifting performance based on the model of sophisticated automotive transmission technology: Perfectly adapted to the intended use and permanently reliable. Virtually wear-free and without adjustment work, the transmission functions with lasting reliability even under the toughest conditions.
