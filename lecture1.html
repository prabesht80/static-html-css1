Bikes

For those who prefer to build their own bike with their own hands, we offer our titanium frames along with advice on assembly. We are also known for making amazing custom racks for our bicycles, as well as hand-built wheels

The Nua Kensho is our sporty and sexy gearbox-equipped urban racer, made to bomb along the city streets in style. It features the low maintenance of a single-speed, but sports the versatility of a sturdy Pinion gearbox paired to a Gates belt drive. To make this extravagant titanium commuter fast and light, we equipped it with a responsive yet comfortable carbon fork and a stealth-black pair of Crank brothers Cobalt wheels.

GEOMETRY & SIZES

WE BUILD BESPOKE BIKES! Currently, all our bikes are custom-made to order and ALL the frames are custom-sized to fit the owner perfectly. Even the geometry can be tuned according to rider preferences and riding style.

So please don't ask us for a sizing or geometry chart. Once a deposit is paid, we will request some measurements and other information, and will work on a specific drawing for your frame/bike.
Nua Bikes
Nua Bikes offers numerous models and (currently) five Pinion frames for various applications. While the Nua Dual and Nua Kensho models are designed for fast, sporty progress in urban surroundings, the Nua Roure, Nua Salvaje and Nua La Bestia are made for travel, adventure and bike packing.
